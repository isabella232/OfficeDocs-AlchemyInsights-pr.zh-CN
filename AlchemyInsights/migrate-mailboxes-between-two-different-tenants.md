# <a name="migrate-mailboxes-between-two-different-tenants"></a>在两个不同的租户之间迁移邮箱

[如何将邮箱从一个租户迁移到另一个租户](https://docs.microsoft.com/Exchange/mailbox-migration/migrate-mailboxes-across-tenants)
