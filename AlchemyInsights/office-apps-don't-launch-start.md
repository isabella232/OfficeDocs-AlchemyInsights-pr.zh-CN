# <a name="microsoft-365-apps-crash-stop-responding-or-dont-launch"></a>Microsoft 365 应用崩溃、停止响应或不启动

若要修复 Microsoft 365 应用挂起或崩溃的问题，请尝试以下选项：

- [调查为](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d#bkmk_addins) Office 安装的外接程序可能的问题
- 检查 [防病毒软件是否与](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d?ocmsassetID#bkmk_conflict) Microsoft 365 应用冲突
- 验证 [或安装 Office 的最新更新](https://support.office.com/article/update-office-and-your-computer-with-microsoft-update-2ab296f3-7f03-43a2-8e50-46de917611c5)
- [使用控制面板](https://support.office.com/article/repair-an-office-application-7821d4b6-7c1d-4205-aa0e-a6b40c5bb88b) 修复 Office
- [卸载](https://support.office.com/article/uninstall-office-from-a-pc-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8) 并重新安装 Office
