有关内容迁移的更多信息，请访问下面的链接。

- [SharePoint 迁移工具](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepointmigration/introducing-the-sharepoint-migration-tool)

- [迁移管理器入门](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepointmigration/mm-get-started)

- [Mover](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepointmigration/mover-plan-migration)
