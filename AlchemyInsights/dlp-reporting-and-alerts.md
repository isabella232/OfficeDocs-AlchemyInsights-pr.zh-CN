**警报：**[配置和查看 DLP 策略警报 (预览) - Microsoft 365合规性|Microsoft Docs](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/compliance/dlp-configure-view-alerts-policies?view=o365-worldwide&preserve-view=true)
